Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment by calling us directly or using our online booking form available on the Contact page.

2. What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations should be made at least 24 hours before your appointment. Late cancellations may result in a service charge.

3. Do you accept walk-ins?

Yes, we accept walk-ins based on availability. However, we recommend booking in advance to secure your slot.

4. What products do you use?

We use premium and skin-safe beauty products from trusted brands to ensure the best results for our clients.

5. How can I contact customer support?

You can reach us via our contact page or email us at support@example.com.
